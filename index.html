CLINICAL EVIDENCE BASE

2D→3D Surgical Imaging: Research Overview

Peer-reviewed evidence supporting AI-powered volumetric reconstruction for surgical applications.

Deep Learning

GAN-Based Reconstruction

Generative Adversarial Networks achieve 98.89% accuracy in 2D-to-3D volumetric reconstruction. Pix2Pix paired translation enables precise intraoperative model generation.

98.89% Validation Accuracy
Ghoshal et al. (2023) · arXiv:2303.09523
Neural Radiance Fields

Intraoperative NeRF Guidance

NeRF-based reconstruction from laparoscopic video + ultrasound achieves <4mm navigation error, meeting clinical safety requirements for liver surgery.

<4mm Navigation Error
Boretto et al. (2024) · IET Research / Nature
Statistical Shape

SSIM Template Deformation

Articulated Statistical Shape and Intensity Models (ASSIMs) learn density from CT databases and fit to X-ray projections for fast, portable reconstruction.

~30s Processing Time
XrayTo3D · IIT Bombay (2015)
Interpolation

Kriging Parallel Processing

Edge-preserved Kriging interpolation with multi-core parallelism reduces 3D reconstruction time by 70% while maintaining >98% shape accuracy.

70% Faster Processing
Zhang et al. (2025) · J. Med. Imaging
